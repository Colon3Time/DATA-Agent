  Olist E-Commerce — Executive Summary
  For C-Suite Decision Makers
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Situation:
Olist operates Brazil's largest e-commerce marketplace connecting 100K+
merchants to customers across 27 states. Revenue is growing 2.3% MoM,
but customer experience varies dramatically by region.

Complication:
• 28% of customers are at-risk of churning (no purchase in 60-90 days)
• North region delivery takes 30+ days vs. 8 days in São Paulo
• Negative reviews are 55% driven by delivery delays
• Top 10% of customers generate ~50% of revenue (concentration risk)

Question:
How can we grow revenue 30%+ while improving customer experience?

Answer — Three Immediate Actions:

🔴 1. Fix Logistics in North/Northeast (R$300-500K upside)
   → Establish fulfilment centres in Recife & Manaus
   → Cut delivery time from 30→12 days
   → Expected ROI: 3-5x in 12 months

🔴 2. Retain At-Risk Customers (R$500K saved)
   → AI-powered re-engagement campaigns
   → Target the 28% at-risk segment
   → Expected ROI: 6x in 6 months

🔴 3. Double Down on Mid-Range Products (R$200-400K growth)
   → R$50-200 products = 45% of orders, 40% of revenue
   → Increase marketing budget + free shipping bundles
   → Expected ROI: 10x in 6 months

Total Projected Impact: R$1.25M — R$1.8M incremental revenue
Total Investment Required: ~R$290K
ROI: 4.3x — 6.2x in 12 months

Recommendation: Approve budget for logistics expansion and customer 
retention programme immediately. Next phase: ML deployment and 
category expansion strategy.
